LET'S PLAY...
You're hidden in the box and that is the perfect opportunity to get into the car and run away!
Goal:
Arrive at the car.
Rules:
Use keys A: right W: ahead, S: back, D: left to arrive at the car. The keys are jumbled for extra confusion!
You will have to restart if you go through any square with a guard or a alarm.
Hint:
The movement is fixed, but the scene is rotating to confuse you!
Alter your texture by pressing keys 1 through 6.
Obtaining the blue orb will enable Water Mode!
The box will transform into a body of water allowing you to pass through the obsticles... HOWEVER heat will cause some water to evaporate.
The body of water is reduced everytime you pass through a lava guard or laser beam. Remaining life is displayed above the box in the form of hearts. Enjoy!
